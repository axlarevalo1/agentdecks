How This Calculator Helps You, the Professional

As a real estate professional, setting the right expectations with buyers is crucial. This Home Affordability Calculator helps you quickly show your clients how much home they can realistically afford, including the impact of different down payment options, debt repayment, and interest rate changes.

⚠️ This tool provides an estimate only. It is not a mortgage approval. For an official pre-approval, clients should connect with a licensed mortgage professional.

Home Affordability Calculator

Disclaimer: This calculator is a tool for informational purposes only and not a substitute for financial advice or pre-approval. GDS is calculated at 39%, TDS at 44% per standard lender guidelines. It includes mortgage principal and interest, estimated heating costs (defaulted to $2,000/year or ~$167/month), monthly condo fees (optional), and property taxes using the most updated online mill rates available, ie 2025 Calgary residential mill rate of 0.0061803. If you are purchasing a condo, please enter your monthly condo fees; otherwise, enter zero. Actual rates and approval will vary by lender and individual circumstances.
